# Controls - Field_GlitchDrumPerformer (Daisy Pod)

## Main Controls

- `Encoder turn`: Select step `1-8`
- `Encoder press`: Cycle parameter page `1-4`
- `Button 1`: Toggle kick at selected step
- `Button 2`: Toggle snare at selected step
- `Button 1 + Button 2 hold`: Fill mode (`Dust` stochastic triggering)

## Knob Pages

- `Page 1`: `Knob 1 = Kick Tone`, `Knob 2 = Kick Decay`
- `Page 2`: `Knob 1 = Snare Tone`, `Knob 2 = Snare Snappy`
- `Page 3`: `Knob 1 = Texture`, `Knob 2 = Grain Color`
- `Page 4`: `Knob 1 = Tempo (70-260 BPM)`, `Knob 2 = Drive`

## LED Meanings

- `LED1`: Step clock pulse, color depends on current parameter page
- `LED2`: Step state at selected step
- `LED2 in fill`: Bright magenta fill indicator
